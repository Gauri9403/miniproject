About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, born on October 15, 1931, was a pioneering scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a central role in India’s civilian space programme and military missile development :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.

Achievements

  • Led India’s first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III) project
  • Directed successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Author of books like Wings of Fire and India 2020
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
